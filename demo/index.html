What RC Pulse does

📊

Health Score

A 0–100 score with letter grade (A–F) based on churn, MRR growth, and refund rate.

💰

KPI Dashboard

MRR, ARR, active subscriptions and revenue — at a glance, no clicking around.

📈

Trend Charts

4 interactive charts: MRR trend, subscription growth, revenue, and growth momentum.

🎯

Benchmarks

Your churn and growth compared against industry averages for B2C subscription apps.

🔒

No server

100% client-side. Data goes directly to the RevenueCat API — nothing is stored or logged.

⚡

CLI Tool

Generate shareable HTML reports from your terminal. Drop into CI/CD workflows.
